Business Meetings

The Americas and Canada

Country Is pre-business chit-chat customary? If there is an agenda, do meetings stick to it? Are interruptions allowed? What kind of communication style is the norm?
Brazil Yes No Yes Direct
Canada No No No Indirect
USA Minimal No No Direct

Direct Communication: You don't need to read between the lines. Communication is honest and blunt- you say exactly what you mean.

Indirect Communication: You don’t want to cause tension and upset your business partner, so instead of saying ‘no’, you say ‘possibly’. This communication style is connected to the concept of ‘saving face’- protecting another’s honour and self esteem.

Dining Etiquette

Follow these tips and never put a hand, fork or word out of place again.

Country Custom
Sweden Look into the eyes of the person being toasted and say ‘skål’ (skohl)
Russia Don’t start drinking until the first toast has been offered
Hong Kong It’s rude to start eating before the host
UAE & India Only the right hand should be used for eating
Switzerland Cut potatoes with a fork, not a knife
Taiwan It’s expected you leave some rice in your bowl
Denmark It’s expected you finish everything on your plate
Brazil Be prepared for lengthy meals (2+ hours for lunch)
South Korea Be prepared to sing a solo karaoke number after dinner
UK Be aware that tea and dinner may mean different things across countries
